When placed on soil between a crop, this will improve the growth speed of this crop. Green particles are emitted to show when the Crop Support speeds up the growth.

To remove a Crop Support, either break the block underneath it or sneak-right-click the block with a [link{item/logistics_configurator}]Logistics Configurator[link{}] or another Crop Support.
